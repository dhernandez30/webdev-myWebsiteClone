Our Mission

Saint Louis Covenant School exists to create a group of adult believers whose faith experience impacts the formation of children as evangelizers and seekers of wisdom and Gospel values. As partners in faith, the school community is committed to academic excellence, a vibrant Catholic spirituality and community service, all for His Glory.
